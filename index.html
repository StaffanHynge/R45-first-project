R45 is a Musichub with 7 equipped studios for you to create your music on. We also have 10 offices where you can have conferences, workshops or just spend your daily officehours in.


Our membership model gives you the chance to collaborate with others, develop your skills in music and marketing and participate in several events and workshops at our spaces. it also gives you the chance to learn and be guided by industry proffessionals.


We have a long history as a record company, studio and school, which has given us classics from different eras and parts of the world.
